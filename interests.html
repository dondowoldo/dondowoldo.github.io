Interests & Hobbies

fitness, roller-skating, travelling, fragrances, ocasional PC gaming, Netflix, and a new hobby I picked up recently - programming.

I have always liked exploring new technologies but never really dived into the deep source where the magic happens.

I knew very little basics of coding from university but it never really excited me. That all changed when I discovered CS50 course lead by professor David J. Malan.

Things that I didn't consider interesting in the past rapidly became a drive for me to learn more and all I can say is that I wish my university teachers approached teaching computer science with the same passion as David does because maybe I would have found out the true career path I wish now to pursue much sooner.
